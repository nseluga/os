# Recruiters & hiring managers

Who reads the portfolio, project writeups, resume, and About page.

## Who they are

Software/ML/data-science recruiters and engineering hiring managers screening
a junior candidate; for the baseball work, MLB analytics staff (R&D analysts,
quantitative analysts) who are themselves technical.

## What they care about

- Evidence over claims: shipped code, real data, measured results, links that work
- Signal in seconds: they skim first — headline findings and concrete numbers up front
- Depth on demand: methodology available for the technical reader who digs in
- Judgment: why choices were made, what failed, what would be done differently

## Tone & rules

- Specific, evidence-driven voice; no marketing language — the full standard
  is the [[reference-portfolio-style-guide]] (`~/os/knowledge/library/style_reference/Project_Writeup.pdf`);
  read it before writing anything they will see
- Never overstate: a junior with honest scoping reads better than one inflating
