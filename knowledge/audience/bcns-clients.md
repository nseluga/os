# bcns clients

Small-business owners the bcns sites/apps are built for (current: Coventry,
DeLucas, L2Detailz — see [[projects/bcns/README|bcns]]).

## Who they are

Non-technical local business owners. They judge the work by how it looks, how
fast it loads on a phone, and whether customers can find what they need — not
by the stack.

## What they care about

- The site working, always — downtime or a broken page is a trust breaker
- Looking professional and current for *their* trade
- Simple asks turned around quickly, without jargon in the conversation

## Tone & rules

- Client-facing writing (site copy, emails, proposals): plain, concrete,
  benefit-first; zero technical vocabulary
- Site content decisions default to the customer's eye, not the developer's
